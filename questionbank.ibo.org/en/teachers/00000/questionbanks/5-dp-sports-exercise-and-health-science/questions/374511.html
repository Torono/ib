Question

The following notational analysis data was collected from the French Tennis Open where Serena Williams played Maria Sharapova. How can it be used to improve performance?

 

A.  Tactical evaluation, technical evaluation, mental relaxation prior to game

B.  Analysis of movement, mental relaxation prior to game, tactical evaluation

C.  Analysis of movement, development of databases, treatment of injuries

D.  Tactical evaluation, technical evaluation, analysis of movement

 

Markscheme

D

Examiners report

Mid-difficulty question with a low discrimination index. Answer A was the main distractor.

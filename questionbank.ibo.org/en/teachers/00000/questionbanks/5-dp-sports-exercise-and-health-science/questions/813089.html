Question

On a windy day, an archer has two minutes to shoot six arrows at an outdoor target. What type of skill is primarily required by the archer?

 

A. Cognitive

B. Perceptual

C. Perceptual–motor

D. Motor

Markscheme

C

Examiners report

Candidates overall did not have any issues with this area and understood that an archer would require good perception as well as motor control for this sort of activity.

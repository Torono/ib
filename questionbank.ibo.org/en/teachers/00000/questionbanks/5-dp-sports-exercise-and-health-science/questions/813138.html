Question

In the sport of curling, team members slide a large stone across ice towards a target. Which is relevant to the stone as it moves across the ice?

 

A. The coefficient of static friction

B. The coefficient of dynamic friction

C. The coefficient of static drag

D. The coefficient of dynamic drag

Markscheme

B

Examiners report

This question was managed well by a large number of candidates, with option A being the most common distractor.

Question

The heart rate data is from a study of younger swimmers (aged 14–16 years) and older swimmers (aged 35–40 years) during a 1500 m swim.

Explain the lower increase of heart rate in older swimmers.

Markscheme

maximum heart rate decreases with age ✔
therefore, upon exercise, older swimmers are closer to their maximal heart rate ✔
depresses the spontaneous electrical activity of the sinoatrial node ✔
the heart becomes less sensitive to adrenaline/epinephrine with age ✔

[3 max]

Examiners report

This was the most challenging question of the paper. Most students who did answer this question partially earned a mark for the recognition that maximum heart rate decreases with age.

Question

What is the predominant fitness component required for high-level performance in shot put?

 

A. Aerobic capacity

B. Agility

C. Muscular endurance

D. Power

Markscheme

D

Examiners report

One of the easiest papers on the paper, a large number of candidates correctly selected D as the correct answer.
